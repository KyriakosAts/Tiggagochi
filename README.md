# Tiggagochi

Tiggagochi is a tiny terminal companion built around Tigga, our little black cat.

It is meant to feel like checking in on a memory you do not want to lose: the quiet look across the room, the small routines, the affection hidden inside ordinary moments. Feeding Tigga, playing for a minute, giving a quick pet, or letting her sleep turns that feeling into something you can revisit instead of just remember.

The app is simple on purpose. Open it, spend a few seconds with Tigga, and satisfy that small nostalgic ache with a living ritual of care.
